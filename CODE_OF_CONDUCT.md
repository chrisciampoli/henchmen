# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for everyone who contributes to or interacts with this project. Treat others the way you would want to be treated: with respect, patience, and good faith.

## Constructive Participation

Disagreements happen. When they do, focus on the technical merits of the discussion. Critique ideas, not people. Assume positive intent unless you have clear evidence otherwise. If you find a problem, bring a proposed solution when you can.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and deliberate intimidation have no place here — in issues, pull requests, code review, or any other project space. Violations will be addressed by the maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct, and to ban contributors whose behavior is harmful to the project community. If you experience or witness a violation, contact the maintainers directly.
